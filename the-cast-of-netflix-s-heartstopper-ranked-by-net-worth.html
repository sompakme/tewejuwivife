The Cast Of Netflix's 'Heartstopper,' Ranked By Net Worth

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, August 4, 2024

One of the newest hits to take over Netflix is the original drama series Heartstopper. This show features two teen boys who are already trying to navigate life in school, but also start to realize that their feelings of friendship may be shifting into something more. As a teen romance, coming of age miniseries, the producers stayed true to their goals in casting. Here is the cast of Heartstopper ranked by net worth.

9 Tobie Donovan (Isaac) Has A $100,000 Net Worth

Tobie Donovan was hired to play the role of “Isaac Henderson” in this television series. He is a beginner in Hollywood, and Heartstopper is officially the first (and as of now—only) credit on his resume. Donovan is represented by Independent Talent Group, which is an agency based in London. Because of how much traction his show picked up, Tobie's net worth is currently at $1K.

8 William Gao (Tao) Has An Estimated Net Worth Of $100,000

William Gao embodies “Tao Xu” in the show, and it may surprise viewers to know that he has no previous acting experience. He is, however, part of a musical group called Wasia Project in which he plays the keyboard and lends background vocals. Because of this success, Gao has a net worth of approximately $100,000.

7 Yasmin Finney (Elle) Is Worth Approx. $200,000

Yasmin Finney has not only been in every Heartstopper episode as “Elle Argent,” but she has since been hired to appear on Doctor Who for an episode that will air in 2023. Besides Hollywood entertainment, Finney is well known on social media as an influencer, both on Instagram and on Tiktok. Both platforms have boosted her standing and net worth to rest at around $200,000.

6 Joe Locke (Charlie) Has A $2-6K Net Worth

Although Joe Locke, who plays “Charlie Spring” on the series, is one of the main characters, his net worth is currently between $200,000 and $600,000. This star is brand-new to onscreen acting with Heartstopper as his first and only filmography credit. His show was just announced to be picked up for two more seasons, so we will be seeing more of Locke on our screens in the next few years.

5 Cormac Hyde-Corrin (Harry) Has Approximately $500,000

Much like the rest of the cast, Cormac Hyde-Corrin is new to acting. He was cast as “Harry Greene” in this series, but his desire is to be a film director. Cormac does not have experience yet, but is attending a school in Great Britain to obtain a diploma in film and media production. With all the work he has put in, his net worth has come to half a million dollars.

4 Rhea Norwood (Imogen) Has A Net Worth Of $2-3 Million

“Imogen Heaney” is played by Rhea Norwood. Heartstopper is her first acting credit, but she has also been cast in an upcoming short called Kill Them with Kindness which is set to premier later this year. Norwood is a proud member of the Bristol Old Vic Theatre School and is also represented by the Independent Talent Group (like her costar Tobie Donovan), bringing her net worth to approximately $2-3 million.

3 Sebastian Croft (Ben) Is Worth Approx. $4 Million

Sebastian Croft has been cast as “Ben Hope” for this series. He is one of the few actors who came into the show with previous onscreen experience, as he has played eleven roles prior to the Heartstopper release, including two episodes of Game of Thrones. Croft also has two movies that are completed and will come out later this year, bringing his net worth to $4 million.

2 Kit Connor (Nick) Has A $5 Million Net Worth

Kit Connor is the second main character of the show, playing the role of “Nick Nelson.” He began acting in 2013 and has added over 20 credits to his filmography, including big names like the movies Rocketman, The Guernsey Literary and Potato Peel Pie Society, and Ready Player One. All the work he put into these titles has raised his net worth to $5 million.

1 Olivia Colman (Sarah) Has A Net Worth Of $8 Million

The most accomplished actor on the Heartstopper cast list is Olivia Colman, who plays “Sarah Nelson.” This English star has been in over 120 productions, including The Crown, Murder on the Orient Express, and all the Thomas & Friends spin-off shows and movies. She also has eight projects currently in the works. Her immaculate performances have given her a net worth of $8 million.

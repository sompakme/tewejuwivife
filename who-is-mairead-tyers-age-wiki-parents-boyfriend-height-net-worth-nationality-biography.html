Mairead Tyers Biography, Wiki, Birthday, and Education

Mairead Tyers was born on January 5, 1998, in Ballinhassig, Cork, Ireland. Presently, she is living in London, England, United Kingdom. Talking about her childhood and qualifications, she spent her entire childhood in Cork and then moved to London.

Mairead went to a Private High School in Cork to complete her schooling. Afterward, she joined the Royal Academy of Dramatic Art (RADA) in 2017 where she got her BA degree in Acting in 2020. Tyers started her acting career by participating in school plays at a young age.

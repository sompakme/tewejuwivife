Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, August 23, 2024

So many questions! 

Miley Cyrus has inspired many with her risky style trends over the years (i.e: her love of high-cut bodysuits and denim shorts), and in that same spirit, she introduced yet another head-turning look on Saturday, April 18.

The “Wrecking Ball” singer, 22, saluted her musical idol, Joan Jett, at the 30th Annual Rock and Roll Hall of Fame Induction Ceremony in Cleveland, Ohio, sporting some serious armpit hair.   

Dressed in a sleeveless black crop top, Cyrus displayed her long armpit hair and assemblage of tattoos at the podium. If her look wasn’t already bold enough, she really woke up the audience with her speech. "I'm going to start off this induction with the first time I wanted to have sex with Joan Jett," she opened.

What Really Happened Between Jana Kramer And Gleb Savchenko?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, July 4, 2024

Quick Links

  • Jana Kramer Had Split From Third Husband Mike Caussin At The Time
  • Gleb Savchenko's Wife Accused Him Of Cheating On Her
  • Gleb Savchenko Denied Reports He Had An Affair With Jana Kramer

Did Jana Kramer have an affair with Gleb Savchenko while they were partnered on Dancing With The Stars in 2016? Multiple sources told Us Weekly that Kramer and Savchenko practiced the "horizontal mamba" off-screen.

Jana Kramer Had Split From Third Husband Mike Caussin At The Time

In 2020, actress and singer Jana Kramer and her Washington Redskins tight end husband Mike Caussin released a joint book called "The Good Fight." Kramer admitted to having some “flings and flirts” when they briefly separated when Caussin went to rehab. The former One Tree Hill actress wrote: “I never truly thought I did anything wrong. Correction. I knew it was wrong; I just didn’t want to be the bad guy. I justified my actions in my mind just as I suppose Mike had done all along too.”

Gleb Savchenko's Wife Accused Him Of Cheating On Her

At the time of Jana Kramer's marital woes, Gleb Savchenko was married to Elena Samodanova. Savchenko and Samodanova - who have two daughters together - announced their split in November 2020. Her rep claimed in December 2020: “It’s no secret that Gleb has had affairs with former dance partners — and who knows who else."

Kramer and Caussin split in 2016 after he allegedly cheated with multiple women and sought treatment for sex addiction. But the pair - who share daughter Jolie, six, and son Jace, three - reconciled in 2017 and renewed their vows in Hawaii. However, they split for good last year, when Jana discovered a woman texted him a topless photo in October 2019.

Jana went on to date former Navy Seal Ian Schinelli, 36, for six months. They broke up in April. The fitness trainer claimed that Jana slept with two men during her 2016 split from husband Mike Caussin. “Jana has admitted to me that she slept with two guys while Mike was in rehab. She slept with Gleb,” Ian told Us Weekly. “She justified it saying, ‘We were legally separated.' She even tried to meet up with Gleb while we were dating."

"He was doing a show in Nashville and she asked me if it was OK if she met up with him. I said, ‘As long as there was nothing there anymore,’ and she said, ‘No.’ She never went and said it was out of respect for me.”

Schinelli previously claimed to Us Weekly that Kramer had an "anxious attachment" to the men in her life. He theorized that it may have been spawned due to her relationship breakdown with Caussin.

Kramer has strenuously denied she had an affair with Savchenko claiming that Schinelli was lying and "twisting the story" in a statement to Us Weekly. "The reason I haven’t said anything further on this breakup was because Ian lied about cheating on his ex-wife and I was trying to protect her. She has become a friend of mine and I was trying to protect her privacy, but she has now given me permission to speak on her behalf."

Kramer went on to add: "Ian is angry because all of his lies have been figured out and is now twisting the story. I have since found out from many women I was not the only one during our relationship, but I’m happily moving on with my life and I hope for him to do the same."

Schinelli responded: "I apologized to my ex-wife and owned my mistakes. … Jana is hurt and is using it against me."

Gleb Savchenko Denied Reports He Had An Affair With Jana Kramer

Meanwhile Gleb Savchenko denied that he had an affair with his celebrity dance partner Jana Kramer while filming Dancing With The Stars. Speaking to Entertainment Tonight, Savchenko shut the rumors down saying "absolutely not" when asked if anything had happened between himself and Kramer.

Related: Jana Kramer Goes On A Date With Kristin Cavallari's Ex-Husband Jay Cutler

"Have we ever had anything? Absolutely not. Absolutely not," he said. "Whatever she might have said it to her boyfriend, maybe she tried to make him jealous, you know. You can ask her the same question, she'll probably answer the same thing."

Kramer has been married three times. In 2004, she was married to Michael Gambino. He was later charged with domestic violence and attempted to kill her. Kramer became engaged to actor Johnathon Schaech, who she had met on the set of Prom Night. They married on July 4, 2010, in Michigan, but separated a month later. She then went on to marry Mike Caussin.

Dakota Johnson Poses Completely Nude In Front of Jamie Dornan For W

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, August 26, 2024

Now it’s gettin’ steamy! Dakota Johnson apparently does get completely naked in Fifty Shades of Grey. In on-set stills shot by the raunchy flick’s director Sam Taylor-Johnson for W magazine, the 25-year-old actress poses completely naked in front of her costar Jamie Dornan.

“[Here] is Dakota Johnson, naked on the floor,” the caption by Taylor-Johnson simply reads on W’s site. In the seductive pic, Dornan — fully clothed — sits in a chair while Johnson strategically covers herself laying naked on a carpet. 

“[This] is the best of my stills. Dakota holds all the power,” the S&M film’s director, who is also an award-winning photographer, wrote alongside another snap of Johnson, this time covered in a bathrobe, and Dornan.

Things then quickly escalate. In another snap, Dornan and Johnson act out a NSFW sex scene, but the image has been cropped PG-13 style for the magazine. (The NSFW version did hit the Internet, though.) “A still from the first lovemaking scene in the movie,” Taylor-Johnson shares. “There were probably 10 to 15 people in the room. It’s an incredibly intimate shot in an unintimate setting.”

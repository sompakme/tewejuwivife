Early Life and Career Beginnings

Jose Zuniga was born on December 13, 1994, in Port St. Lucie, Florida. He comes from an immigrant family from Honduras and grew up with his older brother Juan Zuniga and younger sister Maria Zuniga. Despite humble beginnings, Zuniga always had an entrepreneurial spirit and a determination to succeed.

Throughout his early life, Zuniga explored various business opportunities. At a young age, he started a car wash business and a grass-cutting business. These ventures not only taught him the value of hard work and dedication but also sparked his interest in entrepreneurship.

In 2012, Zuniga made a pivotal career move by starting his YouTube channel “Teachingmensfashion.” Initially, he focused on sharing style tips and men’s grooming content, which led to his channel gaining traction and gaining a growing audience. Zuniga’s charisma, relatability, and genuine passion for fashion propelled him to become a prominent figure in the fashion and lifestyle community.

“I’ve always loved fashion and helping others enhance their personal style. YouTube gave me a platform to share my knowledge and connect with a global audience. It’s been an incredible journey so far,” Zuniga shared.

In addition to his YouTube channel, Zuniga also ventured into business, leveraging his expertise and influence in fashion. He founded ESNTLS, a clothing brand that offers essential wardrobe pieces for men. Additionally, he co-founded other successful businesses including He Grooming, Santa Lucia, and Jade Black.

Who Is Katie Glaser From Below Deck Season 10?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, August 25, 2024

Season 10 of Bravo’s Below Deck is ready to launch in less than a week, with a new cast of characters joining legendary returning cast members. This season also teases Captain Lee Rosbach’s departure from the program. Aside from that, the episode will include St. David, the series’ biggest luxury boat. Katie Glaser is one of the latest recruits to join the series. Katie will make her Below Deck debut as the yacht’s new deckhand. The 31-year-old began her yachting career as a stewardess and worked her way up the ranks.

The synopsis for Below Deck season 10 is as follows:

“Below Deck returns to the Caribbean for its tenth season, captained by sea stud Captain Lee. The crystal-clear seas and strikingly tapered mountains, the Pitons, on the lush island of St. Lucia, are likely to please even the most discriminating visitor.”

The summary goes on to say:

“Aboard St. David, the biggest motor yacht in Below Deck history, the crew is pleased by the sumptuous, gigantic 197-foot vessel, but they rapidly realize that with a larger boat comes more issues.”

With just a few weeks before the new Bravo series premieres, here’s all you need to know about the yacht’s newest deckhand, Katie Glaser.

Katie Glaser of Below Deck is the founder and CEO of Lower Latitudes Yacht Charters.

Season 10 of Below Deck Katie Glaser celebrates her birthday every year in October. Katie’s love involvement with another co-star is also hinted at throughout the season. Katie’s fascination for the glitzy and “fast-paced” yachting sector started when she relocated from Colorado to Ft. Lauderdale, Florida, according to her Bravo profile. Katie’s biography also mentions:

“Katie has worked as a deckhand and first mate on a number of boats since beginning her boating career as a stew three years ago. Katie has worked in a variety of corporate settings, including IT recruitment, account management, and beverage sales, but she has always had a passion for the service business.”

To that, add:

“Katie enjoys snorkeling, wakeboarding, and swimming and spends about as much time in it as she does on it. This outgoing yachtie is always ready for an adventure and is anxious to show she has what it takes.”

Katie also has a strong CV. Her LinkedIn profile states that she is a yacht broker and has been the chief operating officer of Yacht Parties Miami since 2020. Katie is the owner and operator of Lower Latitudes Yacht Charters. In 2018, she was appointed Vice President of Marketing at The Micc Group. Since 2019, she has also served as an operational partner with Southern Yacht Management.

Katie includes the following in her LinkedIn bio:

“My previous and current experiences include promoting and selling items as well as establishing commercial partnerships. I am a customer service professional who understands the value of listening to customers while developing a plan. I am always on the lookout for new experiences and chances in marketing and sales.”

Katie has roughly 3900 followers on Instagram, and with the show airing shortly, her following are sure to grow. According to her Instagram bio:

“Below Deck Season 10 Cast Member @belowdeckbravo @bravotv Boss Babe Mindset Yacht First Mate, Yacht Management, Yacht Charters, Yacht Sales”

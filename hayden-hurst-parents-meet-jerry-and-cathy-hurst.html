Hayden Hurst Parents: Meet Jerry and Cathy Hurst

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, July 25, 2024

Hayden Hurst is an American football tight end for the Cincinnati Bengals of the National Football League.

He played college football at South Carolina and was drafted by the Baltimore Ravens in the first round of the 2018 NFL Draft.

On March 18, 2022, Hayden Hurst signed a 1 year, $3,500,000 contract with the Cincinnati Bengals, including a $2,000,000 signing bonus, $2,000,000 guaranteed, and an average annual salary of $3,500,000.

Before joining the Cincinnati Bengals, he played in the NFL for the Atlanta Falcons.

Hayden Hurst has played 5 seasons for the Ravens, Falcons and Bengals. He has 139 catches for 1,408 yards and has scored 13 touchdowns.

Hayden Hurst Parents: Meet Jerry and Cathy Hurst

Jerry Hurst and Cathy Hurst are the parents of Hayden Hurst.

Jerry Hurst and Cathy Hurst have been married for a couple of years and share two children; Kylie Hurst and Hayden Hurst.

Although Hayden Hurst’s parents’ names have been identified, their details are under wraps.

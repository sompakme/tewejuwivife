    Taylor Kinzler Miss Massachusetts

    Taylor started her pageant career with a victory when she won the 2008 Miss Massachusetts Outstanding Teen Pageant. Moreover, Kinzler has been crowned Miss Massachusetts in 2012 while still a student at the University of Rhode Island where she was studying journalism.

    The Beauty pageant was held at the Hanover Theatre in Worcester. As Miss Massachusetts, she traveled around the state speaking at fundraising and charity events, youth programs, and schools. Furthermore, she highly promoted her youth volunteer initiative known as “Give the Gift of You! Volunteer”.

    Add to that, Taylor also served as an ambassador for the Children’s Miracle Network. The television personality went ahead and represented Massachusetts at the 2013 Miss America Pageant.

    Taylor Kinzler WJAR

    In the year 2011, Taylor interned at WJAR which is based in Cranston, Rhode Island. Kinzler also worked as an intern at WPRI which is in Providence, Rhode Island in the year 2012. In addition to that, she also served as a Staff Reporter at the University of Rhode Island.

    The television host has worked as an intern at The National Press Club in Washington DC; National Geographic Channel in Washington, DC, and WRC in Washington, DC. Afterward, Kinzler became a Freelance Production Assistant at Comcast SportsNet, Bethesda, MD. There, she assisted news anchors with writing highlights and scripts.

    Taylor moved to WABI in Bangor where she worked as a News Anchor/Reporter from May 2015 to May 2018. Currently, Kinzler works at WVIT-TV which is based in West Hartford, Connecticut as a television host and reporter as well as a multimedia journalist.

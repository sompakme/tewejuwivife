Which Cast Member Is The Richest 15 Years Later?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, July 12, 2024

The Holiday came out fifteen years ago. The star-studded cast was no doubt super wealthy back when the film was made, but fans may be wondering how wealthy they are now. From Kate Winslet and Cameron Diaz to Jack Black and Jude Law, which member of the cast of The Holiday has the highest net worth today?

The film took place in both Los Angeles, California, and Surrey, England, and the exterior scenes were filmed in those locations. The interiors were filmed on the Sony Studios lot in Culver City, California. According to IMDb, the film was written with Winslet, Diaz, Black, and Law in mind for the roles they played. Since the film was released, Eli Wallach, who played the role of Arthur, has passed away, so he is not included in this list of the richest cast members from today. Let's see who topped the list!

6 Rufus Sewell Has A Net Worth Of $5 Million

Rufus Sewell, known for his portrayal of Jasper, the man Kate Winslet's character is madly in love with, has a net worth of $5 million. Fans may also know the actor from his other works, including films such as The Father, Judy, and Old. He also played the role of John Smith on the Amazon Prime series, The Man in the High Castle. His older works include appearances in Abraham Lincoln: Vampire Hunter and The Tourist. Prior to his role in The Holiday, he appeared in films such as The Legend of Zorro and The Illusionist.

5 Edward Burns Has A Net Worth Of $40 Million

Edward Burns portrayed the role of Ethan in The Holiday. You know, the guy who gets dumped by Cameron Diaz in the beginning of the film. Even though his role in the film was brief, the actor has ranked up a $40 million net worth over the years. Fans may have seen him in such films as Friends with Kids, Newlyweds, 27 Dresses, and Purple Violets. He also played the role of Terry Muldoon on the TNT series Public Morals in 2015. Prior to his role in The Holiday, the actor had a role in Saving Private Ryan as well as a role in the film She's the One.

4 Jack Black Has A Net Worth Of $50 Million

Jack Black, known for his plethora of comedy movies, as well as for portraying the role of Kate Winslet's love interest, Miles, in The Holiday, has a net worth of $50 million. Since his appearance in The Holiday, the actor has starred in films such as Jumanji: Welcome to the Jungle, as well as Jumanji: The Next Level. He has also done voice work for multiple Kung Fu Panda movies and appeared in films such as Sex Tape, The Big Year, and Bernie. Prior to his work in The Holiday, Black most famously starred in the film School of Rock. He also has a rock band called Tenacious D.

3 Jude Law Has A Net Worth Of $45 Million

Jude Law, who portrayed the role of Diaz's love interest, Graham, in The Holiday, has a net worth of $45 million. Fans may know him from his work as Yon-Rogg in Captain Marvel as well as his role as Album Dumbledore in Fantastic Beasts: The Crimes of Grindelwald. The actor has a total of 77 acting credits listed under his name on IMDb, including the films Side Effects, Anna Karenina, Hugo, Contagion, and a couple of Sherlock Holmes movies. Prior to his work in The Holiday, the actor is credited in films such as All the King's Men, Closer, The Aviator, I Heart Huckabees, and The Talented Mr. Ripley.

2 Kate Winslet Has A Net Worth Of $65 Million

Kate Winslet, known for portraying the role of the highly relatable character of Iris in The Holiday, has a net worth that matches her co-star Law's: $65 million. Mostly known for her role as Rose in the Oscar-winning film, Titanic, the actress has had a stellar career over the years and has won many awards. Most recently she starred in the HBO Max series, Mare of Easttown, for which she won an Emmy. She has also had roles in films such as Steve Jobs, The Dressmaker, and the Divergent series, as well as in critically acclaimed darlings, The Reader and Revolutionary Road.

1 Cameron Diaz Has A Net Worth Of $140 Million

Cameron Diaz has the highest net worth of the entire cast of The Holiday and has a net worth of $140 million. The actress left the world of Hollywood and quit acting after her role as Miss. Hannigan in the 2014 remake of the musical Annie, but she still ranks as the richest member of the cast of The Holiday. She's starred in a ton of movies over the years, including Bad Teacher, What to Expect When You're Expecting, The Mask, There's Something About Mary, Sex Tape, The Other Woman, as well as many, many, others. She had an incredible acting career and now enjoys life as the owner of a wine label, called Avaline.

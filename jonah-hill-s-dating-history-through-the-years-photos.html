Jonah Hill's Dating History Through the Years: Photos

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, September 29, 2024

While Jonah Hill has kept his personal life quiet throughout the years in the spotlight, he has notably been linked to several women, whether it was a brief dalliance or a long-term relationship.

“I’ve had to wait out for people to break up so I can go after a girl that I like,” he told Seventeen magazine in June 2010. “If it’s the right person and you really love them and you feel like it’s right and you believe you’re supposed to be together — you can wait it out.”

At the time, Hill was linked to now-ex Jordan Klein, whom he met in high school. They went on to date for three years.

Following their split, Hill romanced the likes of Rita Ora, Alana Haim and former fiancée Gianna Santos before confirming his relationship with surfer Sarah Brady via Instagram in October 2021.

Hill and Brady made their red carpet debut at the premiere of Don’t Look Up in December 2021. Two months later, he shut down engagement rumors in a since-deleted Instagram post. “The rumors are true,” he wrote, before joking, “I’m engaged. But not to my girlfriend. I am engaged to your mom. I know this is shocking but please respect our privacy at this time.”

It is unknown exactly when the couple called it quits, but Hill was spotted with girlfriend Olivia Millar for the first time in August 2022.

Regardless of his romantic status, Hill has previously asserted that his main goal in life is to be content and fulfilled.

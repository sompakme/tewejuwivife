Alexis Bledel Engaged: Sisterhood of the Traveling Pants Costars Will All Soon Be Married Women!

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, August 22, 2024

Sisterhood of the Traveling Wedding Dress! With Alexis Bledel‘s recent confirmation (via rep) of her engagement to Mad Men costar Vincent Kartheiser, all the members of The Sisterhood of the Traveling Pants — America Ferrera (Carmen), Amber Tamblyn (Tibby), Blake Lively (Bridget) and Bledel (Lena) — will soon be married women.

In the past two years, costars Ferrera, 28, Tamblyn, 29, and Lively, 25, have all tied the knot, and now it’s finally Bledel’s turn. A rep for the 31-year-old actress exclusively confirmed to Us Weekly on Wednesday, March 20 that Bledel is engaged to Kartheiser, 33, after about a year of dating.

And it’s probably safe to say that Bledel will be inviting her former costars to her nuptials. The ladies have represented the Sisterhood at each other’s weddings, and have remained close since starring in the 2005 film and its 2008 sequel, which were based on Ann Brashares‘ bestselling young adult novels.

Here, Us Weekly takes a look back at the weddings of the Sisterhood.

America Ferrera Married June, 27 2011

The Ugly Betty actress was the first member of the Sisterhood to tie the knot to her longtime boyfriend, director Ryan Piers Williams, 31. The couple met when Williams cast Ferrera in a student film at the University of Southern California. Lively and Tamblyn were reportedly in attendance for the intimate ceremony.

Blake Lively Married September 9, 2012

The Gossip Girl star invited all members of the Sisterhood to her surprise, hush-hush wedding to Ryan Reynolds, 36, in Mount Pleasant, South Carolina at Boone Hall Plantation & Gardens. The couple of less than one year exchanged vows in a small ceremony. Lively wore a Marchesa gown with crystal and rose gold embroidery down the aisle, and featured an “overflowing” dessert table at her reception to satisfy her sweet tooth.

Amber Tamblyn Married October 6, 2012

Lively returned the favor the very next month by attending Tamblyn’s nontraditional ceremony to Arrested Development actor David Cross, 48. Wearing a marigold yellow dress and walking barefoot, the House, M.D. actress tied the knot in a woodland setting after arriving via canoe.

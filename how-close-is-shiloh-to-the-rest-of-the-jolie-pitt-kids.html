How Close Is Shiloh To The Rest Of The Jolie-Pitt Kids?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, August 9, 2024

Highlights

  • Shiloh Jolie-Pitt is the only gender-non-conforming member of the family and has a close relationship with their brothers and sisters.
  • Shiloh's sense of style has evolved over the years, but they still have a close bond with their brothers and enjoy running errands with them.
  • Shiloh shares a special bond with their older sister Zahara and looks to her for fashion advice and inspiration.

Somebody's all grown up! Actually, every member of the Jolie-Pitt kid crew is older than you might expect: Maddox is 22-years old, Pax is 19-years old, Zahara is 18-years old, Shiloh is 17-years old, and twins Vivienne and Knox are 15-years old.

They've all got million-dollar net worths, lavish LA mansions to call home, and parents who are permanently on Hollywood's A-list - but some things set Shiloh apart from the rest.

Shiloh is the only gender-non-conforming member of this family, so we'll use 'they/them' pronouns in this article wherever possible. Shiloh is also the first biological child of Brad Pitt and Angelina Jolie, which means they're in a unique position as the oldest kid having to choose between their biological parents in the custody arrangements following Brangelina's ugly divorce.

Does Shiloh share any real common ground with their siblings? Are they staying close to their brothers and sisters in this uncomfortable post-divorce, mid-pandemic climate? Read on to find out for yourself.

Updated October 2023: As Shiloh Jolie Pitt has gotten older, her sense of style and interests have changed. This should not be surprising given that most teenagers waffle on what and who they find interesting.

With these changing interests, the amount that Shiloh once had in common with her brothers may have changed over the years to have less in common as she has gained a love for fashion, like her sister Zahara. But, that does not mean that the close relationship Shiloh had with her brothers has dissipated. Instead, it appears that Shiloh has a close relationship with her brothers and sisters.

With parents as famous as theirs, it is helpful to be able to relate to others who completely understand what that entails. Fortunately, fortunately for Shiloh and her siblings, they have one another, making for a bond that cannot ever be broken.

Shiloh Jolie-Pitt's Close Relationship With The Boys

Jolie has been open about the fact that Shiloh liked to dress like a boy. It was never a secret and letting Shiloh dress how they felt comfortable was always the plan for Jolie and Pitt.

"She likes tracksuits, she likes [regular] suits, she likes to dress like a boy," Jolie explained. "She wants to be a boy. So we had to cut her hair. She likes to wear boys' everything. She thinks she's one of the brothers."

It's 13 years later and Shiloh still gets snapped by paparazzi out running errands with her dad and three bros. Their connection can also be seen in what Shiloh chooses to wear everywhere from red carpet events to casual LA hangs with the family. While some siblings, especially Zahara, often rock glam looks at events with Angelina, Shiloh has been more likely to wear more masculine clothes. However, in more recent years, Shiloh's style has changed to where they wear gowns to red carpet events. Whether this is a permanent change or just experimenting with fashion is unknown.

These days older brother Maddox is usually off at college in Korea. The rest of the kids split time between Jolie's place and outdoor fun at Pitts's Loz Feliz mansion (which has two outdoor swimming pools, a giant trampoline, and a miniature skate park). Safe bet Shiloh and the boys enjoy themselves!

Shiloh Jolie-PItt Takes Robotics Classes With Knox Jolie-Pitt

The Jolie-Pitt boys have a keen interest in science and technology. When he returned to university in South Korea, Maddox reportedly enrolled in biotechnology and life sciences. Even the youngest Brangelina baby has a passion for tech with Knox has been spotted alongside Shiloh attending robotics classes.

What a cool and creative way for this pair to bond! Despite being from a notoriously private family, the siblings have been seen getting accompanied to and from their classes by Jolie's former stunt coordinator, Oliver Crane. Learning to build stuff with a guy who backflips for a living? These kids are living the dream.

Shiloh Jolie-Pitt And Zahara Jolie-Pitt Share A Special Bond

Even though Shiloh seems to have more in common with the boys of the family, they also appear to have an extra-special relationship with their older sister Zahara.

Zahara was adopted by Brad and Angelina just one year before Shiloh came along, making these two the closest siblings in age (besides the twins).

Both Shiloh and Zahara have been involved in soccer for years making PItt proud of their skills. Shiloh has been so active in the sport that they've even been spotted using crutches and an arm sling after some, particularly rough games.

Shiloh also looks to Zahara for styling tips.

"Shiloh loves music, dance, acting and lately, thanks to her sister Zahara, fashion," a source explained. "It wasn't always like that, but now she loves dressing up in her mom's clothes. Zahara is the real fashionista in the family."

It appears that as Shiloh has matured, their relationships with their siblings have evolved with her. As such, it likely varies who Shiloh is closest to depending on where their interests lie from day to day. And given that they come from a large family, Shiloh has options when it comes to who their trusted confidant is as situations arise.

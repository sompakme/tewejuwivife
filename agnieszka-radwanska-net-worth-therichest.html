Agnieszka Radwanska is a Polish professional tennis player with an estimated net worth of $14.7 million.

Agnieszka Radwanska started playing tennis when she was four years old. She turned pro in 2005 when she did an impressive performance during the Wimbledon junior singles.

She was the first Polish tennis player in history to have received the WTA tour singles title in 2007. Radwanska reached the second spot in world's ranking in 2012 when she defeated several opponents such as Julia Gorges during the Dubai Tennis Championships and Maria Kirilenko in Wimbledon.

After three coaches, Radwanska hired former tennis legend, Martina Navratilova as part of her team.

Agnieszka Radwanska was born on March 6, 1989 in Krakow, Poland.

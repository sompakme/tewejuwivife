Migos And Cardi B's Security Team Dodge Charges In Met Gala Incident

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, July 17, 2024

After facing potential charges for a hasty autograph incident at last year’s Met Gala, Cardi B and the Migos‘ security team were cleared of all charges.

According to TMZ, pending charges have been dropped and the case is officially closed. The alleged victim, Daniel Szalkiewicz, refused to speak to the New York Police Department, though there’s footage of Szalkiewicz’s reported assault.

In since-deleted tweets, Cardi B seemingly addressed the incident, affirming she has no issue in signing autographs for over-zealous fans but took a stance against inappropriate moments.

“If you check my tag pics I take a lot of pics with fans,” Cardi said to Pix 11 at the time. “Some people are not fans & sometimes I don’t want no pics and I simply don’t want people too close cause of [pregnant woman emoji] I don’t know what are people’s intentions sooo I Be careful. Why can’t people respect that? [sic]”

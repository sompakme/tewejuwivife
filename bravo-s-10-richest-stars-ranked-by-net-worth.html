Bravo's 10 Richest Stars, Ranked By Net Worth

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, July 17, 2024

Bravo is no stranger to showing the finer things in life. With shows like Million Dollar Listing, Botched, and the ever-popular Real Housewives franchise there's definitely no shortage of glitz and glamour. While the stars of these shows might be rich already, their wealth only increases with their appearances, with some celebrities bringing in insane amounts of money for simply allowing a camera to follow them around from day-to-day.

Related: Ranking The Vanderpump Rules Cast By Net Worth (From Stassi To Lisa)

With so many millionaires on its network, fans are always left wondering who is the richest member of the Bravo family. And just how much are the elite Bravo stars worth?

10 Kandi Burrus - Estimated Net Worth Of $30 Million

Before joining the cast of The Real Housewives of Atlanta during its second season, Kandi Burrus was making a name for herself in the music industry. Kandi was a member of the Xscape girl group the debut during BET's Teen Summit in 1992. In addition to singing with Xcape, Kandi also wrote songs for other artists including "No Scrubs" for TLC. Kandi also went solo in 2000.

With an estimated net worth of $30 million dollars, there's no reason Kandi has to continue working today but she does because she loves it.

9 Jill Zarin - Estimated Net Worth Of $35 Million

Jill Zarin is one of the original cast members of The Real Housewives of New York City. In addition to spending four seasons on the Bravo show, Jill Zarin is also a real estate agent and works alongside her husband as a marketing manager for his fabric and home furnishing businesses, Zarin Fabrics Warehouse and Home Furnishings. She also co-wrote a book with her mother and her sister.

When you combine all of her business endeavors together, Jill's net worth comes out to an estimated $35 million.

8 Yolanda Hadid - Estimated Net Worth Of $45 Million

Yolanda Hadid had a wealth of her own but that didn't stop her from joining the cast of Bravo's The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills during season 3. Though she no longer appears on the series, she hasn't given up reality television altogether and has since been a guest judge on Project Runway and had her very own modeling show.

In addition to her reality television stardom, much of Yolanda's net worth stems from her elusive modeling career thanks to Eileen Ford. She also has worked as an interior designer. It is estimated that Yolanda has a net worth of $45 million.

7 Camille Grammar - Estimated Net Worth Of $50 Million

While Camille Grammar might be most notable for being formally married to Kelsey Grammar, of Cheers and Frasier fame, Camille has also done an amazing job at making a name for herself. In addition to starring on-and-off on The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills series, Camille is also an actress and is part owner of the Grammenet Productions company.

Related: The Real Housewives Of Beverly Hills: All The Times It Was Super Fake

With an estimated net worth of $50 million, no one can argue that Camille isn't a hard worker. In addition to her acting and reality television career, Camille has also worked as a dancer, model, producer, and writer.

6 Heather And Terry Dubrow - Estimated Net Worth Of $50 Million

Neither Heather nor Terry are strangers to the Bravo world of reality TV. Heather appeared on The Real Housewives of Orange Country series for four seasons while her husband, Dr. Terry Dubrow, stars on Botched alongside another Real Housewives husband alum. While these two certainly don't need to stay on reality television with their estimated $50 million wealth it certainly doesn't hurt the bank.

In addition to starring on reality television, Terry works as a very esteemed plastic surgeon in Beverly Hills while Heather has appeared in numerous television shows including Hawaii Five-O and Young & Hungry. Heather also hosts her very own podcast.

5 Carole Radziwill - Estimated Net Worth Of $50 Million

Carole Radziwill spent six seasons as a cast member of The Real Housewives of New York series before she ultimately left the series to return to her career. In addition to starring in the reality series, Carole is a journalist and author. In fact, most of her storylines on RHONY revolved around her writing career. As a journalist, Carole covered everything from gun control to the United States invasion of Afghanistan. She's also a three-time Emmy Award winner and a Peabody winner.

Though Carole might not be thought of as one of the richest stars on Bravo, she actually is. In fact, her estimated net worth is $50 million making her the second richest star of The Real Housewives of New York franchise.

4 Andy Cohen - Estimated Net Worth Of $50 Million

Though some might argue that Andy Cohen really isn't a star of Bravo, we'd have to disagree. Not only is a star behind the scenes serving as a producer for the Real Housewives franchise, but he also hosts Watch What Happens Live on the network. In addition to his time on Bravo, Andy is also a blogger, hosts his own radio show, and is a published author. He's also made appearances as himself on several television shows including Riverdale and Unbreakable Kimmy Schmidt.

Given his insane career history, it should come as no surprise that he is worth an estimated $50 million.

3 Bethenny Frankel - Estimated Net Worth Of $70 Million

While not the richest star of the Bravo family, Bethenny Frankel does earn the title of being the richest housewife of her respective franchise. Bethenny appeared on the first three seasons of The Real Housewives of New York before taking a hiatus. She returned for another five seasons before cutting ties completely. While Bethenny has an affinity for reality television, starring in The Apprentice: Martha Stewart and her own hosting her own talk show, Bethenny is also a successful entrepreneur.

Her television credits along with her Skinnygirl cocktail company has earned Bethenny an estimated net worth of $70 million.

2 Lisa Vanderpump - Estimated Net Worth Of $90 Million

While Lisa Vanderpump's estimated net worth varies, sources believe her net worth currently stands at an estimated $90 million.

Lisa Vanderpump became a household name when she appeared in the first season of The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills. She spent nine seasons on the series before ultimately leaving the show. In addition to appearing on the series, Lisa also stars and executive produces Vanderpump Rules for Bravo and owns several successful restaurants. She is also the founder of the Vanderpump Dog Foundation which has a rescue facility in Los Angeles.

1 Kyle Richards - Estimated Net Worth Of $100 Million

Though in recent years Kyle Richard's net worth has been estimated at being around $50 million, the latest estimate puts her closer to the $100 million mark. With a net worth that high, it appears that she has dethroned former costar Lisa Vanderpump to take the title of richest Bravo star.

Kyle Richards is the last remaining original cast member of Bravo's The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills series. In addition to starring in the series, Kyle is also a former child actress who continued to pursue acting in her adulthood.

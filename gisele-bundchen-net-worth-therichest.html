Brazilian producer, film actress and fashion model Gisele Bundchen has an estimated net worth of $360 million in 2017.

Gisele Caroline Bündchen was born on July 20, 1980 in Três de Maio, Rio Grande do Sul, Brazil. Since 2009, she has been married to football quarterback Tom Brady, with whom she has two children, Benjamin Rein and Vivian Lake.

Gisele Bundchen started getting international prominence as a model in the 1990s, called "The Return of the Sexy Model" in 1999 by Vogue. From 2000 until mid-2007, she was one of Victoria’s Secret Angels. Bundchen is also known to be the pioneer of “horse walk”.

Since 2004, Bundchen has been the world’s highest paid model. In 2007, she was listed as the 16th richest person in the entertainment industry. In the past, she has appeared on the cover of Allure, ELLE, W, Vogue and Harper's Bazaar, as well as on publications like Esquire, GQ, Arena, The Face, Flair, Citizen K, Marie Claire, TIME, Newsweek, Veja, Forbes and Vanity Fair.   In total, she has appeared on the covers of more than 600 magazines.

She has worn the "Red Hot Fantasy Bra," considered the most expensive bra of all time by Victoria’s Secret Fantasy. “Red Hot Fantasy Bra was named by Guiness Book of World Records as the most expensive lingerie that was ever created.

Eventually, she pursued a career in acting and began appearing on films such as Taxi and The Devil Wears Prada.

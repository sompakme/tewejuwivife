A Peek Inside Kevin Hart's Multi-Million-Dollar Watch Collection

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, July 5, 2024

  • Patek Philippe Perpetual Calendar Chronograph - $300,000

  • Rolex Daytona Rainbow - $520,000
  • Audemars Piguet Royal Oak Selfwinding Flying Tourbillon - $560,000
  • Patek Philippe Nautilus 5976/1G-001 - $650,000
  • Patek Philippe Aquanaut Travel Time 5650G - $690,000
  • Patek Philippe Nautilus 5711/111P-001 - $1.1 Million

Kevin Hart is one of the world's most successful comedians, known for his unique brand of humor and larger-than-life personality. He is also known for his love of luxury, reflected in his impressive collection of watches. Hart has a staggering net worth of $450 Million, which allowed him to collect some of the world's most prestigious and iconic timepieces, each with its unique story and value.

Kevin Hart's collection features some of the most prestigious brands in the watchmaking industry, including Rolex, Patek Philippe, and Audemars Piguet.

Audemars Piguet Royal Oak Chronograph - $250,000

One of the standout pieces in Kevin Hart's impressive watch collection is the $250,000 Audemars Piguet Royal Oak Chronograph. The Royal Oak is a rare and exclusive timepiece that will turn heads. With its deep green Grand Tapisserie dial and 18k yellow gold case, this watch is an actual work of art. Only 125 pieces were ever made, and it is part of the exclusive 2021 green collection. The hour markers and Royal Oak hands are finished with yellow gold and luminescent coating, adding an extra layer of visual interest to the already stunning design.

Patek Philippe Perpetual Calendar Chronograph - $300,000

Another unique timepiece in Kevin Hart's impressive collection is the Patek Philippe Perpetual Calendar Chronograph. This elegant, sophisticated watch features a stunning 18k white gold case and a beautiful black dial with gold-tone hands and hour markers. The watch's perpetual calendar function allows for precise tracking of dates and times, while the chronograph feature adds an extra layer of functionality. Kevin Hart’s exclusive Perpetual Calendar is currently valued at a whopping $300,000 as per SCMP.

Patek Philippe Grand Complications Celestial - $385,000

Kevin Hart's Patek Philippe collection includes the intricately designed Grand Complications Celestial 6102R. This watch boasts a decorated black sapphire-crystal dial with a rotating chart of heavenly bodies, displaying the night sky in the northern hemisphere on any given day. The Patek Philippe Celestial is one of the grandest complications of the house, making it stand out from the rest. Its intricate mechanism and 44mm rose gold case make it an exclusive and expensive addition to any collection. The Celestial, estimated to be worth around $385,000, is also owned by Lebron James, as per Wrist Enthusiast.

Rolex Daytona Rainbow - $520,000

A luxury watch collection is complete with a Rolex, and Kevin Hart owns several Rolexes, the most expensive being the $520,000 Rolex Daytona Rainbow. The watch features a 40mm, 18k Everose gold case with a bezel encrusted with multicolored sapphires in a rainbow gradient, as per Montredo. The dial features a black finish with contrasting rose gold accents, and the hour markers are also set with sapphires that complement the bezel perfectly. The watch's chronograph function is powered by Rolex's in-house caliber 4130 movement, which guarantees accuracy and reliability. The bracelet is also made of 18k Everose gold and features a fold-over clasp for security.

Audemars Piguet Royal Oak Selfwinding Flying Tourbillon - $560,000

Kevin Hart's Audemars Piguet Royal Oak Selfwinding Flying Tourbillon is a marvel of horological engineering. The half-a-million dollar watch is housed in a sleek titanium case that measures 41mm in diameter and is fitted with a blue Mega Tapisserie patterned dial. The dial is also adorned with the iconic Royal Oak hands and hour markers, both coated in luminescent material to enhance legibility in low-light conditions. However, the show's real star is the flying tourbillon, which rotates around its axis once every minute to counteract the effects of gravity on timekeeping accuracy. The luxury watch is priced at a whopping $560,000.

Patek Philippe Nautilus 5976/1G-001 - $650,000

One of the most expensive watches in Kevin Hart's collection is the Patek Philippe Nautilus 5976/1G-001, a luxury timepiece that exudes style and sophistication. The 44mm case is made of 18K white gold, while the blue dial features a horizontal embossed pattern and luminous hour markers. The watch also includes a chronograph and date function, powered by the Caliber CH 28-520 C automatic movement. The Nautilus 5976/1G-001 is limited to just 1300 pieces worldwide and is estimated at around $650,000.

Patek Philippe Aquanaut Travel Time 5650G - $690,000

Kevin Hart has a particular affinity for Patek Philipe watches as he owns another in the form of Patek Philippe Aquanaut Travel Time 5650G. Priced at almost $700,000, the Aquanaut 5650G was created to celebrate the 20th anniversary of the Aquanaut collection as per High Time. The unique grenade pattern on the dial is achieved through micro-milling, giving the watch a striking appearance. The 40.8mm white gold case and comfortable rubber strap ensure that this watch is stylish and practical for everyday wear.

Patek Philippe Nautilus 5711/111P-001 - $1.1 Million

The crown prince of Kevin Hart’s spectacular watch collection is the ultra-rare Patek Philippe Nautilus 5711/111P-001. The stunning watch features a deep blue dial that complements the platinum case and bracelet. The Nautilus also features a date display, seconds sub-dial, and luminescent hands and markers, making it easy to read at any time of day. What sets this watch apart is the attention to detail, including the brushed and polished finish of the case and the alternating links on the bracelet. With a limited production run, this watch is highly sought after and is currently valued at a staggering $1.1 Million making it the most expensive watch in Kevin Hart’s collection.

Kevin Hart's impressive watch collection reflects his love for luxury and high-quality timepieces. From classic designs to more modern and bold pieces, each watch in his collection has its unique story and value. Hart has a special affinity for Patek Philippe watches, which is evident from his collection. The limited edition Patek Philippe Nautilus 5711/111P-001 is the most expensive watch owned by Kevin Hart.

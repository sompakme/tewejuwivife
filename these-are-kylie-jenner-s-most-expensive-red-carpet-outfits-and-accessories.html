These Are Kylie Jenners Most Expensive Red Carpet Outfits And Accessories

As a part of the Kardashian-Jenner clan, Kylie Jenner has grown up in the public eye with the show Keeping Up With The Kardashians. Through the years, her style has evolved and matured as she became a renowned businesswoman in Hollywood. With the combination of slip dresses and embellished gowns, the celebrity mogul has shown her influence beyond beauty and made confident choices. While one of the most popular reality stars, Kylie Jenner still makes rare red carpet appearances but attends several family celebrations. As she is a rare sight at public events, Jenner ensures that she makes her appearance memorable.

From her expensive and intricate Met Gala and after-party jewels and accessories to her custom-made dresses for important celebrations like birthdays, Kylie Jenner ensures that every event is celebrated to its fullest. A fashion star for the generation, let’s take a look at Kylie Jenner’s most expensive red carpet outfits and accessories over the years.

2019 Met Gala Jewelry

Committed to the 2019 Met Gala theme of Camp: Notes on Fashion, Kylie Jenner did not hold back with her fashion when she appeared on the pink carpet. According to Vogue, she wore a stunning lilac Versace gown with a matching wig to show a complete transformation. The outfit was made from ostrich feathers, crystal mesh, and Swarovski crystals. All eyes were also drawn to her $4.9 million worth of Lorraine Schwartz 70-carat diamond and sapphire earrings.

18th Birthday Gown

A red carpet-worthy event, Kylie’s 18th birthday celebration was a star-studded event as many famous people gathered for her celebration, including her family. Along with receiving a $320,000 Ferrari as a birthday present, she also stunned in a couture outfit. Celebrating in style, Jenner’s stylist contacted several designers before choosing Nicolas Jebran. Her crystal-embellished dress was made from Swarovski jewels and cost an astonishing $10,000.

2019 Met Gala After-Party Accessories

Kylie Jenner did not stop showcasing her fashion on the red carpet and switched her look for the after-party hosted by Kanye West at Up and Down in New York. Her second custom Versace outfit for the night was an aquamarine bedazzled and feathered cut-out dress, as noted by Harper’s Bazaar. Keeping up with her mermaid-style look, her jewelry featured Paraiba Tourmaline 15-carat drop earrings and several earrings in matching shades that cost upwards of $3.7 million.

Christmas Celebration Jewelry

Kylie Jenner brought old Hollywood glamor to her family’s annual Christmas Eve celebration in 2019. Her stunning green Ralph & Russo gown was a stylish yet daring outfit with a deep thigh-high slit, a fancy bow, and an off-the-shoulder neckline. The mogul drew the most attention to her diamond and emerald encrusted necklace to match the outfit and paired it with emerald heels. While the price of the chain remains undisclosed, it was reportedly worth a fortune.

Wedding Celebrations Gown

Kourtney Kardashian and Travis Barker took their wedding celebrations across the pond in Portofino, Italy, in May 2022. Each Kardashian-Jenner sibling showed their best fashion style, including Kylie Jenner, who dressed in custom Dolce & Gabbana outfits for the festivities. As mentioned by Teen Vogue, Kylie donned a vintage hand-painting dress from 1998 that was reportedly worth more than $10,000. She also wore D&G matching earrings and shoes to complete the look.

Scott Disick’s Birthday Celebrations

In another round of celebrations, Scott Disick’s 37th birthday was a grand celebration in the Kardashian-Jenner household as he celebrated with his family. Kylie was in attendance with her then two-year-old daughter Stormi in a simple white mini dress with a lime green trench coat. Posing with her daughter, what drew everyone’s attention was Jenner’s stunning necklace which Travis Scott gifted in 2017. The diamond butterfly necklace adored on her neck costs upwards of $60,000.

2016 Met Gala

Making her red carpet debut at the Met Gala in 2016, Kylie Jenner dressed her best for the most critical fashion night of the year. Staying with the theme of Manus x Machina: Fashion in an Age of Technology, the reality star custom Balmain sheath dress embellished with crystals, as stated by Seventeen. Her outfit was paired with signature diamond stud Lorraine Schwartz earrings and Aquazzura shoes.

2020 Vanity Fair Oscars party

Making another rare red carpet appearance, Kylie Jenner made heads turn in her Ralph & Russo gown from the Spring Summer 2020 couture collection. The navy tulle strapless gown was studded with crystals, Swarovski xilion-cut, and sequins and came with a structured rounded corset. She later switched the look with a red off-the-shoulder Vivienne Westwood satin gown and layered jewelry from Lorraine Schwartz.

Kylie Jenner has worn many stunning outfits over the years, often seen on her Instagram profile and on her occasional red carpet appearance. The mogul prefers to stay with her favorite designers like Balmain and Ralph & Russo for significant events. She has a close relationship with Lorraine Schwartz to wear enviable, one-of-a-kind jewelry.

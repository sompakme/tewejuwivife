Hilary Duff has been one of the most well known young actresses in Hollywood over the past decade. Yet at the same time, she's also not accomplishing what some of her peers have done. Even those who have come in after her have seen greater success. Meanwhile, she has also succeeded more than others at her same level a few years back. It is likely that she was held back due to a certain situation that kind of got in the way.

Duff had a child and wanted to be around him initially. It took time to get back to work, but like all women with children, she managed to juggle being a mother and even a wife. This on top of acting, being a driver, cleaner, and much more. Apparently, guys, this whole "mother" thing is hard work. Shocking, we know! Duff managed, and then saw an ability to do more.

Duff originally became known through the Disney television show, Lizzie McGuire. This television show was a huge hit for Disney, but it was never going to last forever. This led to Duff being used in other projects for the Mickey Mouse organization, as they saw Hilary as someone they could use for years to come in other Disney material. This led to her addition to the movie Agent Cody Banks, and the DCOM Cadet Kelly. They also went to movie theaters for the first time with an original Disney TV show with the Lizzie McGuire movie.

It did moderately well, but proved the drawing power of Hilary Duff was relatively good. She is now starring in the television show Younger, where she has been since 2015. She has also been a frequent guest host on The Talk. She may be destined for more with the new movie The Haunting of Sharon Tate. Regardless, there is a theory that Duff very well could outshine all other Disney starlets if given an even playing field to do so. When it comes to beauty, Duff is a bombshell. Ultimately, we're going to show off the beauty of Hilary Duff and explain why she may be someone to keep your eye on this year and next year. We hope you enjoy our list of 20 pics that show Hilary Duff will always outshine other Disney girls.

She Can Write As Well As She Looks

Hilary Duff randomly decided to get into writing one day and had a bit of an interest in the writing genre. This led to Duff becoming quite interested in a story that she felt could be told via a novel, which worked out pretty well. With two spin-offs, it does seem likely such an idea could work out. While her writing was useful in book form, she also wrote a fashion column at one point. It seemed like this beautiful young actress, at the time married, was focused on a career behind a computer rather than in front of the camera. While she would end up back in the spotlight again, there was a time when many wondered if she would be. However, if she kept up with impressive literature...we could not fault her for it.

Her Major Disney Channel Original Movie Debut

On Disney Channel, they have something known as a DCOM. This stands for Disney Channel Original Movie, and it's quite a big deal to star in one. Often they use people that are part of major shows on their network, or will soon be part of one. It's uncommon for the star to not get a big offer for a show or already be in one when things get going, but after the High School Musical success, Zac Efron never starred in any other DCOM. But he did appear in some Disney shows off and on. Before this, being part of a DCOM was a significant situation as most were given a big role if they were at the top of the network. For Hilary Duff and Christy Carlson Romano, the two biggest female stars the network had in 2002, the movie Cadet Kelly was their huge opportunity.

Unlike the shows, DCOMs have a certain time limit they have to be filmed in. Shows have a while to get things done, and some even film during the season they are part of. However, a movie is short and sweet. It has to be, as the actors who are part of other shows have to get back to them. This leads to very little downtime for the stars but the movie gives a nice payday. Cadet Kelly was a smash hit for the network and is still voted among the best DCOMs the network ever produced. Duff would not star in another DCOM, but why would she want to? Sometimes, it's okay to be a one-hit wonder.

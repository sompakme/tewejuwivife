Messi, an Argentine professional football player born on June 24, 1987, is recognized as one of the game's all-time greats. He began his career at a young age in his hometown of Rosario, Argentina, before joining the Barcelona youth academy, La Masia, at 13. Messi is well-known for his superb dribbling abilities, close ball control, agility, and field vision. He can score from different positions and is a prolific playmaker, often creating goal-scoring opportunities for his teammates. Messi has represented the Argentina national team in multiple international tournaments, including the FIFA World Cup and the Copa America. Messi earned his first big international prize when Argentina won the Copa America in 2021 and also led the Argentina team to win the World Cup in 2022. Messi has amassed a fortune and is now one of the world's richest footballers.

 Earns: Salary

Messi earns a substantial portion of his wealth through his salary as a professional footballer. He played with Barcelona FC during his professional career, earning one of the highest salaries of any athlete in the world until 2021. However, in August 2021, Messi transferred to Paris Saint-Germain (PSG) following his departure from Barcelona. He also plays for the Argentina national team, receiving a large salary. According to Sportskeeda, Messi earns over $700,000 every week.

Spends: Luxury Cars

Messi is fond of luxury cars and has a collection that includes high-end vehicles such as Ferraris, Audis, and Maseratis. He has been seen driving various expensive cars over the years. Messi has also been photographed driving the Range Rover Vogue, a luxurious and spacious SUV many celebrities favor. Messi has also been driving the Lexus RX 450h, a hybrid luxury SUV known for its comfort, advanced technology, and fuel efficiency. Messi's passion for vehicles has resulted in a fashionable car collection worth more than $35 million.

Earns: Endorsement Deals

Messi has a long-standing relationship with the sportswear brand and has been the face of numerous Adidas campaigns. In 2017, Messi signed a lifetime endorsement deal with Adidas, which was reportedly one of the most lucrative endorsement contracts in the world of sports. Messi also signed various endorsement deals with well-known businesses such as Adidas, PepsiCo, and Huawei, which has resulted in substantial earnings. These endorsements contribute significantly to his overall income. His status as a global football icon has made him an attractive figure for endorsement partnerships.

Spends: Philanthropy

Messi is actively involved in charitable endeavors. Through the Leo Messi Foundation, he has supported various causes, including access to education and healthcare for vulnerable children. Messi has donated significant wealth to charitable organizations and has participated in several fundraising campaigns. He has also started several football academies where he supports young and upcoming footballers.

Earns: Investments and Business Ventures

Messi has made strategic investments and engaged in business ventures that generate additional income. These include investments in real estate, properties and hotels, merchandise store and other commercial ventures. Messi has expanded his business interests into the hospitality industry. Spends: Real Estate

Messi has invested in real estate properties both in Argentina and abroad. He owns multiple properties, including houses in his hometown of Rosario, Argentina, and in Barcelona, Spain. Additionally, he has reportedly invested in properties in other locations, such as Miami, Florida. His huge property investment is estimated to be worth more than $30 million.

Earns: Bonuses and Awards

Messi has earned substantial bonuses and performance incentives based on his individual and team achievements. These include bonuses for winning domestic and international titles, such as league championships and the UEFA Champions League, and individual awards like the Ballon d'Or. He has received multiple awards, which have brought in substantial revenue.

Spends: Lifestyle and Travel

As a high-profile athlete celebrity, Lionel Messi enjoys a lavish lifestyle. He often travels to exclusive destinations, stays in luxury accommodations, and indulges in high-end experiences. Messi owns several luxurious properties, including houses in his hometown of Rosario, Argentina, and in Barcelona, Spain. These properties are often lavish and provide him with a comfortable and private living environment.

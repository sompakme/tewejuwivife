Ariana Grande and Pete Davidson: The Way They Were

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, September 22, 2024

Ariana Grande and Pete Davidson‘s whirlwind romance was over before fans could blink — but the short-lived relationship had many noteworthy moments.

Us Weekly broke the news in June 2018 that Grande and the Saturday Night Live alum were engaged. “They are a perfect fit. They are not rushing to get married,” a source exclusively told Us at the time. “Their friends are really excited and supportive.”

Four months later, Us confirmed that Davidson and Grande called it quits. “Sometimes things just don’t work out, and that’s OK,” Davidson said during a November 2018 SNL episode. “She’s a wonderful, strong person, and I genuinely wish her all the happiness in the world.”

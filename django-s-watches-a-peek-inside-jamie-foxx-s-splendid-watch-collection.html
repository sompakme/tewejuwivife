Singer, actor, and producer Jamie Foxx started out in stand-up comedy but has successfully transitioned into music and cinema. In reality, his performance in the 2004 biographical film Ray earned him an Oscar, a Golden Globe, and a BAFTA. Foxx's mind blowing breakthrough on TV opened new doors for him in music, television, and cinema. He also produced and composed his debut album, Peep This, which was released in 1994. Two years later, he was chosen to star in The Jamie Foxx Show, a five-season television sitcom. Although Foxx played a teenage quarterback who is seemingly cocky yet frightened of failure in Any Given Sunday (1999), his performance in the sex comedy Booty Call (1997) established him as a significant factor. Foxx's portrayal demonstrated that his acting skills went well beyond mere comic impersonations, as did his part as cornerman Drew 0000000 Brown in the biopic Ali (2001).

The estimated net worth of Jamie Foxx is $170 million. In addition to acting and singing, he has also worked as a producer and writer. In a biopic, Jamie will portray the legendary heavyweight fighter, Mike Tyson. He has some Renaissance qualities. He is aware of when to remain calm and when to show off. And the phrase extravagant sums up his watch collection well. As a result, fans will keep an eye out for any watches he wears in the film, but for now, check out his looks on and off-camera.

Diamond Cartier Watch

The American actor, musician, and producer wore a Cartier Santos De Cartier ring that cost $70,000 and is made of 18k two-tone white and yellow gold. For his portrayal of Ray Charles in the 2004 film Ray, Jamie Foxx rose to fame. Since making his acting debut in 1989, Jamie Foxx has performed iconic roles in several movies, garnering both the Academy Award and Golden Globe Award for Best Actor.

Jaeger LeCoultre Grande Reverso Calendar

Jamie also starred in the contemporary rendition of the famous Broadway play Annie. He portrayed William Will Stacks, a successful businessman who aspires to hold public office, as a spoof of the play Daddy Warbucks. His persona was always perfectly attired in suits, and he had the ideal timepiece to go with them: a $23,200 Jaeger LeCoultre Reverso Calendar watch.

Rolex Datejust With Pave Diamonds

An iced-out Rolex Datejust with a blue dial costing $16,361 is the most distinctive and eye-catching timepiece we've seen on Jamie thus far. The entire case, bracelet, and yellow gold center links are covered in ten carats of diamonds, and the blue dial has diamond hour markings.

Rolex President Day-Date In Yellow Gold

Numerous Hollywood celebrities are fond of Rolex and Jamie Foxx is no different. The Rolex President Day-Date is the timepiece Jamie wears all the time. The watch he is wearing is a special edition of 18k yellow gold with a champagne face and diamond hour markers. Although the Rolex Day-Date was designed as a timepiece for formal wear, many people, including Jamie, choose to wear it with casual attire. With a starting price of $12,990, wearing a Day-Date is always a decisive move.

Hublot Watch

The Best Actor Oscar winner Jamie Foxx was spotted wearing a pink gold, diamond-set Hublot watch during the ceremony in Los Angeles. Hublot watches have continued to woo celebrities for the past 25 years. Their unique concept, which combines current technology with revolution and fusion of style, has pioneered the pairing of an expensive gold watch casing with a natural rubber strap costing up to $11,500. Rolex Submariner Ceramic Bezel

The ex beau of Katie Holmes also sports the incredibly functional stainless steel Rolex Submariner model. This timeless timepiece has a water-resistant 40mm Oyster case, a recognizable screw-down crown, a stylish Cerachrom bezel, and a gorgeous black dial. It can be worn while enjoying the great outdoors and making business deals. With a starting price of $8,990, this watch is a mainstay in many watch collections for a good reason.

IWC Portuguese Chrono

The popular 1980s television series Miami Vice was adapted into a movie starring Colin Farrell and Jamie. The film collaborated with IWC to create the $5,290-estimated watches for its characters to portray the contemporary perspective of the television series. Throughout the movie, Jamie wore a black dial IWC Portuguese Chrono; however, for the press tour, he changed to a silver dial IWC Portuguese Chrono with rose gold hour markers. The movie's name is etched on the case back of the limited edition IWC Portuguese Miami Vice watch that was also produced by IWC. There were just 50 of these timepieces produced.
